WritNote - About


About

WritNote is a converter between Skype's markdown language and Markup. It was implemented for the hack.summit() Koding 2016 hackathon.

Credits


We used lexer JS to parse everything., and Iconic's open icon set for the pencil logo.
It was developed by MicrotransactionsMatterToo, who worked on the parser fundamentals, and Crispin Hitchings-Anstice, who developed the website, and created the rest of the parser.
